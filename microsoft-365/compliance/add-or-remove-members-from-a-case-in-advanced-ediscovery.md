1. <span data-ttu-id="ebf0e-106">Sur la page **Advanced eDiscovery** , accédez au cas auquel vous souhaitez ajouter un membre.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ebf0e-106">On the **Advanced eDiscovery** page, go to the case that you want to add a member to.</span></span>

3. <span data-ttu-id="ebf0e-108">Cliquez sur **Mise à jour**.</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="ebf0e-108">Click **Update**.</span></span>
